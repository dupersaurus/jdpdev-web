fencing-ref fencing scoring and timing

The app attempts to provide the referee full access to the basic functionalty of bout management without various shortcomings that all other apps present, while providing aid and cues otherwise found on more advanced scoring machines. The app presents a UI where important functionality are easily accessible, and the chance of mis-hits are minimized or eliminated.

Check it out

Main display gives quick and obvious access to the main functions. Tap the timer to start and tap the scores to increment. Access to other actions -- priority, period break, and medical timeout -- are easily available, but with minimal chance to mis-hit.
It's obvious when the timer is running, and it's easy to stop: tap anywhere on the screen, no way to go wrong.
Timers share a similar look and feel, but it's handy to be able to see the score at times.
